Stay private online with
our high-speed VPN

MEGA VPN combines security and speed to ensure a great experience while browsing, downloading files, and more. Available for Android and included with all MEGA Pro plans.

Browse securely and safely

MEGA VPN encrypts your internet connection and masks your IP address so no one can snoop on your online activity.

Advanced ChaCha20 encryption means your browsing is always secure and protected against prying eyes, even if you’re connected to an unsecured public Wi-Fi network.

Connect multiple devices

Protect your privacy online, wherever you are. Install MEGA VPN on up to five devices, including your smartphone and tablet.

MEGA VPN is currently available for Android and will be available for iOS, Windows, and macOS devices soon.

Your privacy is our priority

How and what you browse online is nobody’s business but yours.

We don’t log or share your browsing activity, DNS queries, browser type, or the time you spend on pages.

Around the globe access

Connect to 12 locations across the world, including in North America, Europe, Asia, and New Zealand. We’re working to add more locations soon.

Seamless private connectivity

If your VPN connection drops unexpectedly, our kill switch will ensure your device is automatically and instantly disconnected from the internet. Your IP address and other sensitive information remain shielded at all times.

Lightning-fast speeds

MEGA VPN uses the advanced WireGuard protocol to deliver speeds that outpace older protocols. Connect in an instant, browse without waiting, and download large files in record time.

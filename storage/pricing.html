Why store your data with us

Your data is private

No MEGA data ever leaves an individual’s device unless it has been encrypted in such a way that it can only be processed by the user, or intended recipients.

Your data is secure

MEGA protects your data from online attacks with zero-knowledge encryption. Simply put – your data is encrypted and only you hold the keys.

Easily access your data

Easily access your files through our desktop app, mobile apps or the web, anywhere and anytime.

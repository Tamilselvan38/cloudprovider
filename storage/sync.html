Easy, automated
synchronisation

Sync your device and MEGA using MEGA Desktop App.
It’s as easy as a few clicks.

Easily access the latest version of your files anywhere, anytime

Any changes to the synced files on your computer will automatically and instantly apply to MEGA or vice versa, so you can access the most up-to-date versions of these files from any device by logging into MEGA.

Sync the folders shared with you to enhance collaboration

Collaborate on your computer

When a user shares a synced folder with you, you can sync this folder with your computer. This way, you and the person who shared the folder can collaborate on it on your computers, and the changes, such as adding, deleting, moving, or editing files within the folder, will be automatically applied to MEGA and the other person’s computer.

Sync the way you want

Sync all your MEGA folders or just some

You can choose to sync your entire MEGA Cloud drive with a folder on your computer, or select specific folders to sync.

Filter your syncs

Create filtered syncs by excluding certain file names, file sizes, or file types, such as images or PDF files, in your synced folders.

Collaborate easily
and seamlessly

Secure syncing

With MEGA’s zero-knowledge encryption, your synced files are stored securely in MEGA. Only you will have access to them and will be able to see or read them.

Sync multiple computers with MEGA at the same time

Sync as many computers as you want simultaneously with MEGA. Any modifications you make to a synced folder on one computer will automatically apply to MEGA and other computers.

Compatibility

Sync on Windows, macOS, or Linux, or even between different operating systems. For example, if you’re on macOS, you can share synced folders with someone who will sync these folders on Windows.

Browser integration

After installing the MEGA Desktop App, you can set up syncs from MEGA on your browser and the folder will be synced as soon as the app is running.
